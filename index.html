Flight

A freeing movement sandbox where you take control of a bird by moving each wing individually.

Movement and Physics Programming:
Combines the input of two joysticks to determine which direction you are turning, up down left right, along with finely tuned movement functions that allows players to trade height for velocity and vice versa. All intentionally crafted to meet the pillars of freedom and mastery of movement.
